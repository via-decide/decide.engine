Get ViaDecide on
any device.

Install our Progressive Web App — works on Android, iPhone, Windows, Mac, and Linux. No app store needed. Works offline too.

1
Open in your browser — Chrome, Safari, Edge, or Firefox
2
Tap the install prompt — or use your browser's menu
3
Enjoy offline access — fast, native-like experience on your homescreen
📱 Android — Chrome
  1. Open viadecide.com in Chrome
  2. Tap the 3-dot menu (⋮) at the top-right
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. Tap Install — done! Icon appears on your homescreen.
🍎 iPhone / iPad — Safari
  1. Open viadecide.com in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share button (□↑) at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add — the ViaDecide icon appears on your homescreen!
💻 Desktop — Chrome / Edge
  1. Open viadecide.com in Chrome or Edge
  2. Look for the install icon ⊕ in the address bar (right side)
  3. Or open the 3-dot menu → "Install ViaDecide"
  4. Click Install — opens as its own app window!
