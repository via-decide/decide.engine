03 · Process

Research → Synthesize → Decide.

One workflow. Four sources. One structured output. Built for the IndiaAI era.

01
Enter your query

Type any topic or decision question. ViaDecide searches Wikipedia, Wikivoyage, Wikibooks and Wikinews simultaneously — multi-source, multilingual, attributed.

02
Synthesize & structure

The engine extracts thesis, key takeaways and contradictions from each source. You get a structured decision-ready brief, not a wall of text.

03
Export & decide

Export as a policy-grade PDF brief with attribution. Share links, generate quizzes or embed outputs into your own workflow via Engine License.
