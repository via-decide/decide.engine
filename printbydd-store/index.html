📱 Android — Chrome
  1. Open store.viadecide.com/printbydd in Chrome
  2. Tap the 3-dot menu (⋮) at the top-right
  3. Tap "Add to Home screen" or "Install app"
  4. Tap Install — the PrintByDD icon appears on your homescreen.
🍎 iPhone / iPad — Safari
  1. Open store.viadecide.com/printbydd in Safari (not Chrome)
  2. Tap the Share button (□↑) at the bottom of the screen
  3. Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. Tap Add — the PrintByDD icon appears on your homescreen!
💻 Desktop — Chrome / Edge
  1. Open store.viadecide.com/printbydd in Chrome or Edge
  2. Look for the install icon ⊕ in the address bar (right side)
  3. Or open 3-dot menu → "Install PrintByDD"
  4. Click Install — opens as its own app window!
